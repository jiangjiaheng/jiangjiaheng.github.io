# Daily English Words

  1. zeal (n): They plunged into their work with immense zeal
  2. Swallow (vt): If you swallow something, you cause it to go from your mouth down into your stomach.
  3. Health (n): My health is not too bad
  4. Healthy (adj): I want to have a healthy life
  5. disdain (vt): I disdain you
  6. Forecast (n): The weather forecast says that tomorrow is sunny day
  7. Outright (adv): she rejected his proposal outright
  8. Pervade (vt): the sick smell pervade every part of this room
  9. Specialize (vi): I specialize in computer programs
  10. Deliver (vt): he delivers the package to his client
  11. Banknote (n): these banknotes come in various denominations
  12. Hitchhike (vi): the girl sitting on the roadside is trying to hitchhike
  13. Misdeed (n): misdeed means crime
  14. duplicate (by): Don't duplicate what you already do.
  15. Examine (vt): Examine the flow of each step.
  16. Exam (n): His exam results were good.
  17. Example (n): for example
  18. swift (adj): swift means fast
  19. Triangle (n): three angles
  20. foreign (adj): we’re foreigners in foreign countries
  21. Barber (n): in a barbershop, a barber is cutting my hair
  22. Undercharge (n): the vendor undercharged me by ten yuans
  23. Superintend (by): the man superintend me
  24. Accompany (vt): my mother always accompanied me go to school in the past
  25. Infirmary (n): infirmary is a small hospital
  26. Sponsor (n): thanks to all the sponsors for the support
  27. The couple (n): the couple means a wife and husband
  28. Withstand (vt): we all withstand all kinds of pressure
  29. check in or out: she has to check out at noon
  30. Seclude or include (vt): seclude is the opposite of include
  31. Feel like doing (by): use the phrase of feel like doing to instead of want to do
  32. Take sth in (vt): the girl has to take in her dress a little to make it fit better
  33. courier (n): the job of courier is to deliver the parcels
  34. On account of(adv): use it instead of because of
  35. Postpone (vt): I had to postpone or put off the meeting until later
